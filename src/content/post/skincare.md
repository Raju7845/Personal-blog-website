Taking care of my skin was never something I prioritized earlier.

But about a year ago, I started my skincare journey — and it completely changed how I look at health, lifestyle, and self-care.

This is not just about products.  
It’s about understanding what truly works.

---

## Where It All Started

To be honest, I used to believe that skin color or glow is something that cannot be changed.

At that time:
- I was overweight  
- I didn’t focus much on food habits  
- I had no proper skincare routine  

But everything started changing when I began focusing on my health and reducing weight.

As my weight reduced, my insulin levels improved — and surprisingly, my skin also started to glow naturally.

That’s when I realized: skincare is not just external, it starts from within.

---

## My First Skincare Routine

When I started, I had very basic knowledge.  
Most of what I learned came from YouTube and Google research.

My initial routine included:

1. **De-tan face wash**  
   - I chose this mainly to remove tanning  
   - Picked it based on what suited my skin  

2. **Basic essentials I learned about**  
   - Moisturizer (Himalaya)  
   - Serum (Deconstruct)  
   - Sunscreen (Deconstruct)  

I followed this routine consistently for around **6 months**.

And yes — I started noticing visible glow.

---

## The Biggest Realization

At first, I thought:

> “These products are the reason my skin is glowing.”

But later, I understood the truth.

The real reason was:
- Cutting down sugar  
- Improving diet  
- Better lifestyle  

That’s when I came to a simple conclusion:

- **70% of skin improvement comes from food and lifestyle**  
- **30% comes from skincare products**

This changed everything for me.

---

## Understanding What Works (and What Doesn’t)

As I continued experimenting, I realized:

- Not all products suit everyone  
- Even popular products may not work for your skin  

For me:
- Some moisturizers didn’t suit well  
- Certain sunscreens felt uncomfortable  

So I started upgrading my routine based on what my skin actually needed.

## A Simple Skincare Framework

If you are just starting, don’t overcomplicate things.

Focus on these essentials:

1. Facewash  
2. Vitamin C (for glow)  
3. Moisturizer (for hydration)  
4. Sunscreen (for protection)  

If you stay consistent with just these, your chances of seeing results are very high.

---

## Final Thoughts

This journey taught me something very important:

Skincare is not about expensive products.  
It’s about consistency, understanding your body, and making the right lifestyle choices.

Your glow is built from:
- What you eat  
- How you live  
- And how you take care of yourself
